How It Works

Simple process, no surprises.

We keep it straightforward. No long onboarding. No big process overhead. Just get talking, agree on scope, and start.

01

Tell us what you're thinking

Share the idea, the problem, or even just a rough direction. We'll ask a few questions to understand what you actually need — and whether AI is even the right tool for it.

02

Agree on scope

We keep scope realistic. A POC, a specific feature, a short engagement — we'll agree on what's being built, how long it takes, and what done looks like.

03

Build and iterate

We start building and share progress regularly. If something needs to change mid-way, that's fine — we stay in sync and adjust without drama.
